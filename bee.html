NPR Rooftop Beehives

I participated in "Serendipity Days," a three day challenge to come up with a project that would somehow benefit NPR. I decided to make logos for the two NPR rooftop beehives, Swarming Edition and All Stings Considered.

Logos

The logos were designed as signage for the rooftop beehives and as stickers for the NPR Gift Shop. I was inspired by vintage seed boxes, brooches, and flat iconography. The complimentary color palettes and shapes can be expanded upon if more beehives are ever added to the rooftop.
