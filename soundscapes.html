Brand Soundscapes

Brand Soundscapes are shorts pieces of audio created in-house by the custom sponsorship team at National Public Media, NPM Creative, for NPR sponsors. I received brand assets from clients sucha as logos, fonts, color palettes, and imagery, and used those to build banners that met NPR's sponsorship guidelines. My team developed new products and adapted existing products to support these placements on NPR's platforms. Learn more about Brand Soundscapes and listen to examples.

NPR.org

On npr.org story pages, two placements display the Soundscape as a video. The larger banner is a 250px high, responsive with banner that appears within the body of the article on the page. The second banner is a 300x600 static banner on the right side of the page. If a user plays a video in either banner, the other banner changes to a call-to-action focused design.

Client: Target

Client: Jack Daniel's

NPR App

The Brand Soundscapes placement for the NPR App is a custom, responsive width banner that plays the Soundscape audio in the app's player.

Client: Westin
