Hirshhorn Museum Brand Identity

The Hirshhorn is a contemporary art museum in Washington, DC. I designed new brand assets which included a logo, a color palette, a calendar for upcoming events, and a poster series for current exhibitions.

Logo

The logo, a stylized H, takes inspiration from the windows seen from the inner walls of the museum.

Calendar

A small, portable calender informs visitors of non-exhibit related events at the museum such as films and lectures. The round shape is interactive and also resembles the architecture of the museum.

Poster Series

The layout of the posters is inspired by the same architectural elements as the logo. The colors and imagery for each poster reflect the exhibition it is promoting.
