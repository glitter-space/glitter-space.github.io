RAIN Podcast Summit

I developed a visual language and designed various environmental assets for NPR's presence at the RAIN Podcast Summit in New York.

Concept and Headline

Working from the headline This is Audio, Elevated. This is NPR, for Brands, I designed a visual concept based on topographical maps. The colors and typography draw on NPR's brand guidelines, and the background graphic is a custom illustration.

The Wall

The largest asset was an 8-foot by 42-foot wall featuring the headline and key graphic.

Stage Banners

Two 3-foot by 7-foot banners were displayed on the event stage in the venue.

Lobby Screens

Vertical screens throughout the lobby displayed supporting headlines with a reverse color scheme. Additional screens with the dark color scheme were projected on a backlit wall.

Street Banners

Two 2-foot by 6-foot banners were displayed outside the venue.

Elevator Banners

These roughly 2-foot by 6-foot banners were displayed near the elevators in the venue.
