Jaguar Land Rover Retail Opening

I worked with a copywriter to develop key visuals and headlines for dealerships across Germany who were building new Jaguar, Land Rover, or dual brand locations.

The Cat

The concept for Jaguar was inspired by the phrase "We've let the cat out of the bag," the German translation of which was used for the headline. We presented two options for key visuals to the client. The first was a Jaguar F-Type that is mostly in shadow except for its headlights. The second was an image of an empty bag with paw prints walking away from the opening.

The Suit

The Land Rover concept was developed around the headline "Dirty. Sexy. British." and suggests that a Land Rover is still professional even when it gets dirty. The key visual is of a man wearing a suit that is splashed with mud as though he had been driving off-road with the car's windows open.

The Road

The concept for the dual brand dealership used the headline "On Or Off, The Most Important Thing Is The Road." The key visul was based on the layout of dual branded ads, which depict a Jaguar car on the left and a Land Rover car on the right, separated by a white margin. Instead of cars for this concept, the two brands are represented by a paved city street and a gravel road in the countryside.
